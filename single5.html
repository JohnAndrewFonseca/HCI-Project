Mechanical Keyboard hobby, What are they?

Mechanical keyboard hobby is certainly one of the niche hobbies out there, those within the community have a passion centered around customizing their own keyboards. Though an expensive hobby, enthusiasts are willing spend money just to reach their satisfaction.

In case you don't know mechanical keyboards are different from the common computer keyboards. Common keyboards uses a rubber or some sort of silicone beneath the keys, that when you press on it is quiet, much unlike mechanical keyboards which has mechanical switches beneath it providing a more tactile and responsive typing experience.

As stated earlier, this hobby is quite expensive, factors that contributes to this are, high-quality materials used, customization options, and also the amount of money you splurged on this hobby. Being financially stable and responsible is certainly a must before entering this hobby.

Overall, this hobby offers a unique blend of creativity, craftmanship, and technical expertise. The satisfaction it gives after you make something your own is a good feeling that not only improves our mood but our mental health as well.
